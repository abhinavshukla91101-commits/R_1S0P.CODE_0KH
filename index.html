// ARCHIVE LOG 01: SIMULATION PROFILE

Dream? is a solo-developed, atmospheric psychological survival horror game built in Unreal Engine. Straying away from typical monster-chase tropes, the game plunges players into the infinite, mind-numbing isolation of Level 0. Your greatest enemy isn't a creature lurking in the shadows—it is your own failing body and the crushing weight of solitude.

[ DEHYDRATION METRICS ]

Scavenge for scattered bottles of Almond Water to slow physical crash loops. Every drop matters.

[ PSYCHE FRAGMENTATION ]

Corridor saturation induces visual distortion, hallucinations, and deep environmental paranoia.
